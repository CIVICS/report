Payment

The payment functionality of the Kansas Business Center is integrated with the KanPay Payment Portal and allows payment either by major credit card or e-Check. From the KanPay website: “KanPay is an online payment processing system specifically designed for state and municipal governments to process both credit card and e-check payments via the Internet.” See http://kanpay.kansas.gov/Pages/default.aspx for more information.
